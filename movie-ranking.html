The Best Movies Accoriding to Hemanth


My Top 3 movies of all time!

1.KUSHI

Kushi (2001) is a Telugu-language romantic comedy film written and directed by S. J. Suryah, and produced by A. M. Rathnam. The film stars Pawan Kalyan and Bhumika Chawla. Mani Sharma provided the score and soundtrack. The film was released on 27 April 2001 and was a major commercial success.

2.RANGASTALAM

Rangasthalam is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language period action drama film written and directed by Sukumar. Produced by Y. Naveen, Y. Ravi Shankar and C. V. Mohan for the company Mythri Movie Makers, the film stars Ram Charan and Samantha Ruth Prabhu with Aadhi Pinisetty, Jagapathi Babu, Prakash Raj, Naresh, and Anasuya Bharadwaj in key supporting roles. Rangasthalam is set in the eponymous fictional village in the 1980s.

3.BAAHUBALI

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli and produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under Arka Media Works. It is the sequel to the 2015 film Baahubali: The Beginning, and stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, and Tamannaah. The film tells the story of Amarendra Baahubali's son, Mahendra Baahubali, as he fights to reclaim his kingdom from his uncle Bhallaladeva.
